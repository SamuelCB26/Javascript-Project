About me...

The beginning

Fields, flat land, woodlands, dilapidated village halls, cheap alcohol, home made posters, cheap amps, cheesey lyrics, second-hand guitars, clapped out vans, smoking in cafes, underage drinking, karaoke on Tuesdays, house parties, police raids, street corners and no care in the world...

Inspiration

My dad passed away in the summer of 2019. The country went into lockdown early 2020. Life can be really shit. However, music has a magical ability to help you process, express and reflect. In the summer of 2020 I sat in my home studio (which I had recently painted a desert orange) and started writing the first song to this album, Cannonball.

..there's a better life..

Sam FanDango

Orange Room was the first single realeased and it got featured as BBC Intro Lincolnshire's song of the week. The songs were a collection of feelings, musings, experiences over the past two years. There was and is still no plans for this little collection of songs, just a snapshot into a period of my life for me. However, if you do find yourself listening, I hope you enjoy them and take a little piece of me with you. Peace and love, SFD x
